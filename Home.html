Sun and Moon

Moon

The only place beyond Earth that humans have explored,
the Moon is the largest and brightest object in our sky
– responsible for the tides
and keeping Earth stable on its axis.

Sun

The Sun is the heart of our solar system
and its gravity is what keeps every
planet and particle in orbit.
This yellow dwarf star is just one of billions
like it across the Milky Way galaxy.
